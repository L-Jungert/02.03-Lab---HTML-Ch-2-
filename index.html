Boise, Idaho

Location

Boise is located in southwestern Idaho, along the Boise River and near the Boise Foothills. It is part of the Treasure Valley and is the capital city of Idaho.

About

Outdoor Lifestyle

Boise is known for its easy access to outdoor activities. The city has trails, parks, and the Boise River Greenbelt, which makes it a great place for biking, walking, and spending time outside.

Downtown

Downtown Boise has restaurants, shops, events, and local businesses. It has a fun city feel while still being close to nature.

Culture

Boise has a strong local culture, including music, food, sports, and a well-known Basque community. The city feels active, creative, and welcoming.

Things To Do

  • Hike or bike up in the foothills!
  • Visit the nightlife/restaurants in downtown!
  • Go skiing or snowboarding at Bogus Basin!
